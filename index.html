Court Process/Fees

We know that dealing with the Criminal Justice process can be very confusing, time consuming and expensive. We can guide you through the basics of your specific case and explain your charges as well at what they mean. We will also help you gain represention if you are unable to aford it or it is declined by the court.

Post Release Job Search

When released from Prison/Jail we know that a large ammount of former inmates have a hard time adjusting back into society.
